Special Screenings

A number of special screenings or events take place at the Riverview. Please be aware that there may be a different admission price or policy in effect for these screenings. Our regular film programming schedule runs from Friday-Thursday. Because of an occasional special event, our schedule can change from day to day, so it is best to check the schedule for the particular day you plan to attend.

The Man Who Saves the World?

The Man Who Saves the World? follows acclaimed director Gabe Polsky as he gets swept into the orbit of spiritual journeyman Patrick McCollum, it sparks a globe-spanning, transformative adventure. Along the way, they cross paths with legends like Jane Goodall and Chief Phil Lane Jr., as Polsky uncovers a mission that might just be crazy enough to save the planet. Tuesday, November 18 at 7:00pm. Admission is $15. Director Gabe Polsky and Patrick McCollum will be here for a post-film conversation.
